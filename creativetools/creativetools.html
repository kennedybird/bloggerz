How To Help Yourself Out?

Pinterest is a great way to collect, organize, and sift through ideas. I love it because once you save to boards your feed is entirely catered to you. I like to make boards catered to various crafting ideas that I have. Another form tool that is easy to use is cutting up pieces of magazines or random pieces of scrap and gluing them into a junk journal. You can accumulate this over time as you find stuff, then it makes looking back at it super fun!
