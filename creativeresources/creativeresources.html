Additional Tips & Resources To Help On Your Crafting Journey

Creative Tools

There are lots of really cool tools you can use to help you out with your creative process. No shame in getting some help. I personally have a hard time getting started with crafting sometimes but getting some inspiration can help get things moving. Pinterest is one of my forever favorites, as well as making a journal where I can draw or glue in inspo.

Creative Spaces

For me, there are some places that manifest more creativity than others. I wanted to give some inspiration of places I've been to when I've crafted & see if that inspires others to seek out places that give them a good feeling too.
